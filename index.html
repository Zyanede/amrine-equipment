About

Welcome!

Over 40 years of experience in the heart of downtown Clayton, GA. With 10,000 square feet of floor space, we are dedicated to all of your equipment needs.

Our employees are trained and certified mechanics for the repair of a range of equipment - over 250 types of equipment.

Services

From massive construction equipment to hand tools and lawn equipment, we offer a full range of equipment rentals and sales to help homeowners, contractors, and anyone else do the jobs that need to be done. All of our equipment is reliable and well-maintained, and we will work closely with our customers to recommend proper equipment for their needs. Customer satisfaction is our #1 priority.

Construction Equipment
For big jobs, we have a full range of construction equipment available for sale or rent. Both new and used equipment is available. We have everything you need for your job.

Garden Equipment
Keep your lawn in order with our garden equipment. We have everything you need to have the nicest lawn on the block.

Miscellaneous Equipment
From automotive equipment to industrial equipment, you can rent or buy whatever you need to get your construction project finished. A huge amount of equipment is available for rent.

Motorcycle & ATV

We offer sales, service, parts, accessories, and customization. Our mechanics are highly skilled and have years of experience. Most of all: they love bikes and ATVs and we care about yours. We’re committed to repairing your ride and to serving it with quality and value. Bring your bike or ATV in today for an inspection, and see what our commitment to quality is all about!
